Plant came in dead on arrival?


Please follow the unboxing protocol and record yourself in a well lit room unboxing your plant(s) and photograph the conditon your plant(s). Place in a bowl of water and in a humid area. Document plant status over the course of a week. If plant shows no signs of growth in the present or the future, you are eligible for a refund or a replacement plan.
